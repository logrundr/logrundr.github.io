Four Couplets IV

Poem 011, 2023/2/19–3/3


XVII (2023/02/19)
Ocean waves crashed on the rocks outside
as soft music was played and the piano sighed.


XVIII (2023/03/02)
Wild branches and mangled roots are what made this tree
and still it lives just as the rest do—paying no mind to its lack of beauty.


XIX (2023/03/02)
His clothes were ragged, and sagged and dragged on the ground.
He lived on the street, and got them from Lost & Found.


XX (2023/03/03)
“No one will ever love you more than you do.”
“That’s obvious”, you exclaim sarcastically, “Who knew?”
