Summer Night

Poem 001, 2021/12/31


The wind blows as I look through the window,
over the hill and through the trees.
The city glows in its soft light below,
across the river separating us.

I drift my eyes upwards towards the sky:
dark grey clouds float there,
floating in front of a black July.

I can tell that she is looking too,
over the hill and through the trees.
What does she think of the view,
the night, and the summer breeze?

I tell her that it’s becoming late.
Her response:
don’t leave me. Stay here and wait.
