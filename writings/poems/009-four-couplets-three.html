XII (2023/02/06)
Leaves fall off the tree, landing in sunder
as people crunch over them, wishing they were younger.


XIII (2023/02/17)
Let me tell you how I overcame my introversion:
I noticed that everyone around me was only a person.


XV (2023/02/17)
The ones who preach goodness are themselves the most evil;
Unable to change themselves, they set about changing other people


XVI (2023/02/17)
Everything is already decided, you must admit.
Will Fate drag you, or will you walk beside it?
