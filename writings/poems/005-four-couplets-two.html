Four Couplets II

Poem 005, 2023/1/23–2/3


VI (2023/01/23)
Never before has a poem ever been written
which describes the human soul with which we are so smitten.


IX (2023/01/26)
Well if the first of man to love was He,
Then what was love as knew by chimp and ‘key?


X (2023/01/27)
Slugs wore armor once upon a time
when they were snails with shells of swirly spine


XI (2023/02/03)
They say that love is like a gravity:
it attracts, and bends, and distorts reality.
