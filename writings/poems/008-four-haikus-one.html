Four Haikus I

Poem 008, 2023/1/26–2/25


I (2023/01/26)
Man on a lake
in a cold winter morning
rowing as the sun rises


III (2023/02/01)
Dark skies and wet pavement,
headlights cutting through rain,
soft music on radio


IV (2023/02/19)
Black sky and dark clouds,
bright stars shine there—the moon too;
A beautiful night.


V (2023/02/25)
Morning after a rain—
Droplets rest on grass blades,
And trees shake off the water.
