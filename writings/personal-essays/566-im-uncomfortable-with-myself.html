I'm Uncomfortable With Myself

Entry 566, 2023/2/7


I feel like I am so far behind everyone else: I have no friends, I don't go to clubs, I'm entirely a recluse, I have no girlfriend, I can't cook, I can't do anything independently, and my personality didn't start to really develop until the very end of high school, which seems very late compared to everyone else. There's this overwhelming sense that I'm not the right person for myself. I'm not comfortable with how I am or who I amâ€”not yet, at least. I want to be a different person. I want to be more social, and more charming, and have more hobbies, and be more driven and motivated.

At the same time I feel disconnected from reality. I go to class every day and I don't even remember going; I play piano and it feels like it's not my hands which are playing but someone else's; I think about asking out a girl that I have a crush on and it feels like I'm in a dream and that it could never be reality. Anything that I think about feels like a dream, even if it actually happened.

How do I change myself? How do I make myself better?

I want to be a better person, but I don't know exactly who and I don't know how to get there. All I know is that I'm uncomfortable being myself and I'm tired of it.

Here is a quatrain which I wrote today:

Quatrain III
All things have their end and are born again,
so wishing you were young is an undoing.
To waste all your life is the sin of man,
so love your Self in happiness pursuing.

Ideally, love would come to me. But knowing life, I think I will have to go find it.
