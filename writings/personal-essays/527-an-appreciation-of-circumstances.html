An Appreciation of Circumstances

Entry 527, 2021/10/4


I am very grateful for everything that my life has been.

My parents are good to me. People are good to me. I've generally been quite popular throughout my school years. I've had relationships that stopped before things went too far and I regretted something, which I probably wasn't happy about at the time but I am now.

My life so far has been very quiet. And I'm thankful that I am who I am, because I like who I am and who I am becoming. I've grown a lot over the duration of the quarantine (since March 2020), and will probably continue to grow as I age. I like my development and everything that has happened to me so far in life.

I have no regrets. I have no difficult memories. I think it's hard to be grateful for thingsâ€”it's much easier to complain about everything that's wrong.

All circumstances are just a result of a large butterfly effect. There is no such thing as something that doesn't happen as a result of something. In other words, there are no independent events. Every event is dependent on something that happened before it.

I'm grateful that my butterfly effect has so far been very positive. Life could be a lot better I think, but mine has been better than most.
