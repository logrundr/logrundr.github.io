(554) What I Value, 2022/5/16

“ Creativity to me is seeking the new; it’s the opposite of stagnation. Creativity to me is not just the drive to create but the drive to learn and experience as well, because learning facilitates creation. In my view, stagnation is the ultimate life failure. ”


(552) I wish I could leave society, 2022/4/18

(548) Sometimes the end comes too soon, but never on accident, 2022/3/20

“ There have been times in my life that I felt that the end came too soon. I felt it after {my first girlfriend} broke up with me. I felt it during my 8th grade graduation. I felt it when the pandemic cut my senior year short.

But there is something invigorating about having a new beginning; about starting fresh and having a clean canvas; about being able to do whatever you want, because now you are free from your past self. ”
