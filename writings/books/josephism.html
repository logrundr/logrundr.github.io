The Book of Josephism

1st Book, Chapter 1 — The Universe, Our Creator


1 Everything has existed for eternity. The universe made it so since the universe created itself an eternity ago. 2 Time is infinite in both directions. There is an eternity before us and an eternity after us. 3 All of eternity has happened, and our fates and actions and words are already sealed. It is we who live it but can’t change it; we, the creations of the universe. 4 The universe can change our fate. It can decide it, but the ones without the universe cannot.

5 The universe lives through us and within us. The universe lives without us but always with us. The universe is within us, so we are the universe. 6 The universe can change our fate, so we can also change it. 7 We are the universe and we created eternity, and we can change eternity and our own fates through the hand of the universe that exists inside us all. 8 Notice it within you and you have the power of all of nature.
