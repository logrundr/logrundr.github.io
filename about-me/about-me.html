About Me


I'm Aidan, a 20-year-old college student majoring in astrophysics. I enjoy writing and keep a journal which I write in semi-frequently. This site has a page with excerpts from that journal. I'm also an amateur musician; I play organ, piano, and guitar, and am learning music theory and composing.

I am interested in becoming a polymath. There are many fields which I intend to study in the coming years.

Please send any inquiries, questions, or comments about my work to logrundr@proton.me. Also let me know of any errors if you spot them. You could also email me if you simply want to chat, especially if you've read my writings on this website and find yourself feeling similarly to me.
