Original Project Proposal

1. Give a brief synopsis of your project’s argument, a few sentences to a paragraph should do.

I want my project to send a message about animal rights through several media simultaneously. My idea is to write a narrative poem (or at least some form of somewhat long poetry) that is accompanied by piano, as well as illustrations depicting imagery from the poem, similar to a comic. My main argument will be simple: animal cruelty is bad. I think most people would agree with that sentiment, but I want my poem to expose people’s hypocrisy, since most people that agree with that also directly support animal cruelty themselves (through eating meat, dairy, etc.)

2. How does your project advance the audience’s knowledge of an issue?

I’m hoping that it will cause some introspection and make people realise that we treat different species of animals differently for no good reason.

Why did you select your particular media?

I was originally going to compose a piece for only solo piano, but I’m not confident enough in my playing nor my composing abilities. I then got the idea that I could add words to give more meaning and take some of the focus away from the piano. I’m not 100% sure that I’ll be adding the illustrations. It’s an interesting idea that I could take in a lot of different directions, and I like doodling. I could possibly even do a stop-motion animation using hand-drawn illustrations if I have the time.

3. Detail the practical steps you need to take to complete the project, i.e. detail your creative process. What materials or equipment is necessary? Provide a rough schedule for your project.

(1) Write the long-form poem and map out where any significant changes in the music occur; (2) Record the piano part following cues in the poem; (3) Narrate the poem following the piano accompaniment to make sure rhythm is correct; (4) Possibly repeat steps 2 or 3 until components are good; (5) Create illustrations; (6) Piece together a video presentation that shows all components together simultaneously.
