Board Game

a simple card-based strategy board game

Art Project 002, 2022/5/23


Rules

Setup

  • The board is a 7x7 grid
  • The castle is placed in the center of the back row of each side
  • 3 pieces are placed on either side of each castle (to fill the back row), and 3 are placed in front of it

Cards

  • The players’ decks are identical and consist of the following moves, for a total of 15 cards:
    • 2 single-space cardinal, 2 single-space diagonal
    • 2 double-space cardinal, 2 double-space diagonal
    • 1 triple-space cardinal, 1 triple-space diagonal
    • 1 uni-diagonal left, 1 uni-diagonal right
    • 1 bi-diagonal
    • 2 horizontals
  • 3 cards in hand
  • May choose to place cards (all 3) in reserve (takes turn)
  • Gets a free move (1 unit adjacent) and card moves
  • Draw cards to get back to 3 at end of turn
  • For use of reserve, discard 3 cards in hand and take the 3 reserve (takes turn)

Play

  • The castle can be moved with the free move, not with cards, and can move 1 unit adjacent
  • Pieces are not allowed to jump over other pieces
  • Play begins with the free move, where any piece may be moved 1 unit adjacent
  • After the free move has been played, a card may be used on any other piece (cannot be used on the piece that the free move was used on)
  • If a piece is captured within the turn (using either the free move or the card move), then the player gets a bonus move
    • Can be skipped if the player doesn’t want to move again
    • Uses a card
    • If another piece is taken within the bonus move, another bonus move is not granted
    • The bonus move can be used on any piece, including ones previously moved within the turn

Capturing

  • Land a piece on an enemy occupied space to capture the enemy piece
  • If the castle is captured, the game is over
