Two-Year Calendar (B02B):
The Seasonal Calendar Version 3

Assume all properties of B02A apply to this calendar, except for the changes listed here:

  1. Length: 730 days
  2. Leap years: Every 2 cycles, except on multiples of 50 unless also a multiple of 200
  3. 8 months, which are the names of months 1-4 and 13-16 in B02 and B02A.
    • The months are the same lengths as B02A.
    • Month names from Italian seasons and Latin words for “First” and “Last”.
  4. The holidays and event dates listed in B02AA and shown in the Seasonal Calendar are still valid, and occur on the same day of the same season, except occurring twice as often (since the cycle is twice as short).

Optionally, the year can be 4 months long, making it the standard 365 days.

  1. Length: 365 days (730 days)
  2. Leap Years: Every 2 cycles of 8 months. Every 4 cycles of 4 months.
  3. The holidays can be celebrated every 4 months (365 days), as is normal in the Gregorian calendar.
  4. There would still be 8 month names, to be more specific about what day of the week it is (since the days of the week corresponding to the dates repeat every 8 months).
