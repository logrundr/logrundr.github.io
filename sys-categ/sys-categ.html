The categorisation is constructed as follows:

  1. The main category
  2. Two digits after to signify the number within its category (in chronological order from date of creation, so D01 is the first thing created within the category D, "Languages")
  3. After the two digits, a letter--from A to Z--to indicate subfolders of its parent categorisation
  4. Additional letters indicate subfolders of the subfolder
