Welcome to my website. I mainly post personal writings and projects.

My Personal Essays are excerpts from my personal journal. They are not polished writings; I don't spend long on them, and I write them primarily for my own sake. My Articles are written over a longer period, perhaps a week or two; include links to research that back up my claims; and are written for a larger audience as a persuasive or informative piece.

Note that any artwork used within the "Writings" section on the left navigation bar is not my own artwork (unless stated that it is mine), and typically will fall under the public domain. This includes paintings and drawings within the Poems, and occasional images within the Personal Essays, and possibly other things. Everything other than the artworks is my own original work.
